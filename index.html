About Us

An informal school registered with corporate affairs commission (RC No. 1600378) to operate in Nigeria. The school shall be known as DANKAWU ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND VOCATIONAL SCHOOLS established in the Year 2019 by the Directors of Dankawu Entrepreneurship and Vocational Schools Ltd. With Abdulrahman Mustapha Baba as Chairman Board of school Directors, as well as the chief promoter of the school, contacts can be made at arcambaba@gmail.com/devovationalschools@gmail.com, or telephone Numbers 08065848177, 08021090192, C/O Dankawu Universal Services ltd at Suit C 1 Duwa Plaza Plot No. 7 Opposite Bauchi Motor Park Gombe.

As an Independent school intends to start operation at ANHAMNAN Complex Adjacent Darul Arqam Federal Low Cost Off Goodluck Ebele Jonathan Road Gombe, Gombe State. The school to expressly address the phenomenon of rising youth unemployment and its threat to political stability, social cohesion and economic growth of the nation. As a school we have adopts a person-Centered and demand -Driven approach towards promoting and developing competent and productive initiatives in the area of small enterprises.

Objectives

The objective of the school is targeted at unskilled and unemployed youths both male and female to equip and up-skill them with relevant demand-driven skills.

Mission

We are committed to empowering people and adding value to their lives by teaching vocational crafts and trades in a practical, innovations, flexible, interactive and motivating environment.

Vision

Be the number one in vocational training and business resource Centre in Nigeria and raise self-dependent entrepreneurs’ that will reduce unemployment in our country.
